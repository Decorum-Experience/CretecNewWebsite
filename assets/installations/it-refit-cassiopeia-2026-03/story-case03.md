IT Refit on a 37m Classic Wooden Sailing Yacht (Built in 1946) 

When modern IT meets maritime tradition:
Refit of an approximately 15-year-old IT system on board a beautiful classic sailing yacht.

Initial situation:
A grown, functional system – but no longer aligned with today’s requirements. The goal was to significantly improve performance, transparency, and operational reliability without compromising the unique character of the yacht.

Our implementation:
✅ Completely newly structured IT rack (patch panel, switche, router)
✅ Clean and clearly organized cabling
✅ Integration of modern network and communication solutions
✅ System design focused on easy operation by the crew
✅ Scalable architecture for future expansions

Connectivity – 3 levels for maximum availability:
1️⃣ Marina WiFi
2️⃣ LTE/5G Router
3️⃣ Backup LTE Router with preparation for a future upgrade to Starlink
➡️ Automated prioritization ensures stable connectivity – in port and at sea

Rethinking digital navigation:
The ECDIS environment was enhanced with a Didi Chart Server
➡️ Automated provisioning and updates of nautical charts
➡️ No more manual processes for delivering/preparation charts to the ECDIS
➡️ Increased safety through always up-to-date ENC´s

Special highlight:
External antenna solution on deck – cleanly installed and perfectly aligned with the requirements of this beautiful classic sailing yacht 

Result:
- Clear structure and full transparency within the rack
- Significantly increased reliability
- Stable, high-performance connectivity
- Automated chart management without manual intervention
- A system that can be managed independently by the crew

Future-proof IT infrastructure
➡️ Modern technology – integrated with respect for a true classic.
